Understanding payout policies

EMI Transactions

Commissions of 2.2% calculated on net value. Disbursed post partner check-in triggers (T+3 working days).

Payment Gateway (PG)

Commissions of 1.8% calculated on transactional success. Disbursed daily on T+1 settlement cycles.

Mandate (Auto-debit)

Flat ₹15 per successful debit + 0.5% net value. Disbursed in monthly batches on the 10th of every month.
